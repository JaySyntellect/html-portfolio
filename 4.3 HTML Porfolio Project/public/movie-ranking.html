GOAT Bollywood Movies Of All Time

Top 3 Movies of All Time

3 Idiots

Directed by Rajkumar Hirani, "3 Idiots" is a comedy-drama film that follows the story of three engineering students at an Indian engineering college, Rancho, Raju, and Farhan. The movie explores themes of friendship, the pressure of societal expectations, and the flaws in the education system. Rancho, played by Aamir Khan, challenges the conventional methods of teaching and encourages his friends to pursue their passions. Through humor and emotion, the film delivers a powerful message about the importance of following one's dreams and finding true happiness.

12th Fail

"12th Fail" is a Bollywood film released in 2021, directed by Atul Sabharwal and produced by Anil Kapoor, and co-produced by Rhea Kapoor and Harshvardhan Kapoor. The film falls under the genre of drama and comedy, and it addresses themes of education, societal pressure, and self-discovery.

Ferrari ki Sawaari

"Ferrari Ki Sawaari" is a heartwarming Bollywood film released in 2012, directed by Rajesh Mapuskar and produced by Vidhu Vinod Chopra. The movie tells the story of a father's unconditional love for his son and the sacrifices he makes to fulfill his son's dreams.
